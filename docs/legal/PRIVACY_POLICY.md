## 1. Introduction

**ness.** ("we," "our," or "us") operates the n.process platform (the "Service"), accessible at https://nprocess.ness.com.br. n.process is a multi-tenant SaaS platform for intelligent compliance management that enables organizations to map business processes, analyze compliance with regulatory frameworks, and manage compliance operations using artificial intelligence.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, company name, and password
- **Profile Information**: Role, preferences, and settings
- **Content**: Documents, processes, compliance data, and other content you upload or create
- **Communication**: Messages, support requests, and feedback

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our Service:

- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, features used, time spent, and actions taken
- **Device Information**: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
- **Log Data**: Access times, error logs, and performance metrics
- **Cookies and Tracking**: Cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies

### 2.3 Third-Party Authentication (Google Sign-In)

**Purpose of Data Collection**: We use Google Sign-In to provide you with a secure and convenient way to access our Service. This eliminates the need to create and remember a separate password for our platform.

**Data We Request from Google**: When you choose to sign in with Google, we request the following information:

- **Profile Information**: Your name and email address (required for account creation and identification)
- **Profile Picture**: Your Google profile picture (optional, used to personalize your account)
- **Authentication Tokens**: Required for maintaining your authenticated session

**How We Use Google Data**: 
- We use your Google profile information (name and email) to create and manage your account
- Your email address is used as your unique identifier and for communication purposes
- Your profile picture is used to personalize your account interface
- Authentication tokens are used solely to maintain your login session and are not stored permanently

**Data Sharing**: We do not share your Google account information with third parties except as described in Section 5 (Data Sharing and Disclosure) of this Privacy Policy.

**Your Control**: You can revoke our access to your Google account at any time through your Google Account settings (https://myaccount.google.com/permissions). Revoking access will log you out of our Service, but your account data will be retained as described in Section 11 (Data Retention).
